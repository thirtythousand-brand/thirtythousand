Investor enquiries

投资洽谈

Snapshot

Essence: effortless, time-saving wardrobe essentials for globally mobile professionals (28–45).

核心：为 28–45 岁的全球流动型专业人士打造省时、有章法的高端基础衣橱。

Launch line: Hero Tee 220gsm organic cotton (longer back hem, side vent); Indigo-dyed tote.

首发系列：主打 220gsm 有机棉 T 恤（后摆加长、侧开衩）；手工靛蓝染托特包。

Go-to-market: Instagram + Shopify, waitlist-led drops, pop-ups in Hong Kong / Brisbane / Singapore.

市场路径：Instagram 与 Shopify 先行，候补名单限量发售；香港 / 布里斯班 / 新加坡快闪。

Positioning: “quiet luxury” basics — COS/Uniqlo U silhouettes, Everlane-like transparency.

定位：“低调奢华”基础款——版型参考 COS / Uniqlo U，透明度参考 Everlane。

Why now

Customer pull

Fewer, better pieces with responsible provenance; consistent fit that saves time.

消费者正在寻找“少而精”的负责任产品；稳定的版型让穿衣更省时。

Lean supply

Audited partners in South China; yarns from South China/Turkey; capped runs ≤ 300 per colour.

中国华南审核工厂；纱线来源中国南方 / 土耳其；单色限量 ≤ 300 件。

Price integrity

Small-batch scarcity supports margin; transparency builds trust and repeat.

小批量稀缺支撑毛利；透明度提升信任与复购。

Product & Proof

Fabric benchmarks: 220gsm organic cotton, double-needle stitching, pre-shrunk (≤ 3% after 30 washes).

面料基线：220gsm 有机棉，双针缝制，预缩处理（30 次水洗收缩 ≤ 3%）。

Design signatures: longer back hem, clean side vent with discreet woven tag.

设计标识：后摆加长、利落侧开衩，低调织唛。

Brand world: airport-ready minimalism; indigo craft; studio journal to show process.

品牌世界：机场即穿的极简风；靛蓝染工艺；工作室日志呈现过程。

Targets & Economics

12-month targets

Revenue ≥ AUD 40k; ≥ 600 units; ≥ 3k IG followers; ≥ 1k email; repeat > 25%.

12 个月目标：营收 ≥ 4 万澳元；销量 ≥ 600 件；IG 关注 ≥ 3,000；邮件名单 ≥ 1,000；复购率 > 25%。

Budget (AUD 20–22k)

Sampling/R&D ~3k; First inventory 3–7k; 3PL setup 3k; Paid content/ads 7k; Contingency 2k.

预算（约 2–2.2 万澳元）：打样/研发 3k；首批库存 3–7k；第三方仓 3k；内容/投放 7k；预备金 2k。

Unit economics (illustrative)

COGS discipline via capped runs, direct-to-consumer margin, and minimal SKU complexity.

单位经济性（示意）：限量控制与直连消费者提升毛利，SKU 精简降低复杂度。

Use of Funds & Milestones

Use of funds: finalize fits/fabrics, produce first drop, content sprints, waitlist growth, 3PL setup (Shenzhen/HK).

资金用途：定型版型与面料、生产首批、内容冲刺、增长候补名单、搭建深圳/香港 3PL。

Milestones: P0 sampling ✓; P1 fit approval → P2 production order; Drop 1 live; Post-drop learnings → Drop 2.

里程碑：P0 打样 ✓；P1 版型确认 → P2 生产下单；首发上线；复盘优化 → 第二次限量。

Detailed deck and financial model available upon request.

可按需提供详细路演材料与财务模型。

Risks & Mitigation

Inventory risk

Mitigated via capped runs, waitlist-led demand, and conservative colourway count.

库存风险：通过限量、候补名单导向与克制配色数量来缓解。

Fit acceptance

Iterative sampling, small early cohorts, measurement transparency.

版型适配：迭代打样，小规模早期用户测试，尺寸透明。

Ops scaling

3PL-first model; staged SKU expansion only after repeat cohorts validate.

运营扩张：以 3PL 为先；仅在复购群体验证后分阶段扩 SKU。
