Investor enquiries

投资洽谈

Snapshot / 项目一览

Essence: effortless, time-saving wardrobe essentials for professionals aged 28–45 who live between airports, boardrooms, and cities.

核心：为 28–45 岁、往返于机场与会议室之间的专业人士打造省时、有章法的高端基础衣橱。

Launch line: Hero Tee in 220gsm organic cotton (longer back hem, side vent) and an indigo-dyed tote that carries the brand story.

首发系列：主打 220gsm 有机棉 T 恤（后摆加长、侧开衩），以及承载品牌故事的靛蓝染托特包。

Go-to-market: Instagram and Shopify first, waitlist-led drops, and focused pop-ups in Hong Kong, Brisbane, and Singapore.

Positioning: quiet-luxury basics — COS / Uniqlo U silhouettes with Everlane-level transparency.

市场路径：以 Instagram 与 Shopify 为起点，通过候补名单限量发售，并在香港、布里斯班、新加坡举办定向快闪。

定位：“低调奢华”的基础款——版型参考 COS / Uniqlo U，透明度对标 Everlane。

Why now

为何是现在

Customer pull / 需求驱动

Customers are shifting toward fewer, better pieces, natural fibres, and consistent fits that save time and mental load.

消费者正在转向“少而精”：偏好天然面料、稳定版型和不用费心搭配的衣橱体系。

Lean supply / 精简供应

Audited partners in South China with capped runs of ≤ 300 units per colour and yarns sourced from South China and Turkey.

华南经审核工厂生产，单色限量不超过 300 件，纱线来自华南与土耳其的可靠供应商。

Price integrity / 价格与价值

Small-batch scarcity supports margin, while sourcing transparency builds trust and repeat purchase.

小批量带来合理溢价，透明溯源增强信任，从而推动复购。

Investment case / 投资逻辑

Thirty Thousand is a lean, founder-led operation with a functioning supply chain, tested prototypes, and early community traction.

The brand is already structured to grow profitably without external capital, compounding steadily as a focused, design-led label.

Thirty Thousand 由创始人亲自主导，已搭建完整供应链，完成产品测试，并积累首批早期拥护者。

在没有外部资金的前提下，品牌亦可保持盈利并稳步复利增长，作为一间专注设计的精致小众品牌持续前行。

With aligned investment, the growth curve changes: a single injection of around USD 0.5m accelerates reach, product depth, and marketing efficiency.

Under the projections in our prospectus, that level of capital unlocks an eight-fold annual return by Years 3–4, assuming disciplined execution and realistic sell-through.

在有理念一致的投资加持下，增长曲线将明显改变：约 50 万美元的一次性投入，可迅速放大触达范围、产品深度与营销效率。

根据路演材料中的测算，在稳健执行与合理售罄率假设下，该水平的投入有望在第 3–4 年实现约 8 倍年回报。

Our structure is built for long-term stability. Hong Kong anchors our design IP and administration, early manufacturing runs leverage trusted South China partners, and future premium-sourcing initiatives in Australia give the brand additional depth and resilience.

This balanced footprint supports strategic growth while offering investors exposure to a diversified, internationally grounded operation.

我们的架构着眼长期稳健：设计知识产权与行政管理立足香港，早期生产依托华南值得信赖的合作工厂，未来在澳大利亚布局高端面料与小批量生产，为品牌增加深度与抗风险能力。

这种均衡布局既支撑品牌的战略扩张，也为投资者提供来源多元、根基国际化的资产机会。
